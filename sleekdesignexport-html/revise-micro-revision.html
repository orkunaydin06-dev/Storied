Micro-revisionHow it could be told

Start with the exact sensory detail. Not what happened — where you were, what you could see.

Try something like:

  • • "The coffee was still hot when I sat down."
  • • "It was 9:47 on a Monday."
  • • "I remember the rain."

Open on that one detail. The story follows.
